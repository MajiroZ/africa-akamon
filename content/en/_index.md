## Welcome to Africa Akamon-kai

**Africa Akamon-kai** (東京大学アフリカ同窓会 – 赤門会) is the Pan-African Alumni Association of The University of Tokyo, a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting and supporting University of Tokyo alumni across the African continent.

We work to build networks, foster collaboration between Japan and Africa, and advance the educational mission of The University of Tokyo throughout Africa.

### Our Mission

- Build networks among University of Tokyo alumni across Africa
- Support current students and early-career researchers
- Promote Japanese–African academic, business, and social collaboration
- Strengthen ties between the University of Tokyo and African institutions
