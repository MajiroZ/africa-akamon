## Events & Activities

Africa Akamon-kai organizes events at both continental and national chapter levels to connect alumni, foster collaboration, and advance our mission.

---

### Annual General Meeting

The Annual General Meeting (AGM) is held between January and March each year, rotating among countries where chapters have been established, to encourage broad participation and strengthen pan-African connections.

The AGM includes:
- Reports from the Continental Board and national chapters
- Election of officers and board members
- Discussion of continental initiatives and strategy
- Networking and collaboration opportunities among members from different countries

---

### Chapter Events

National chapters organize:
- Local networking events and social gatherings
- Professional development activities
- Support for University of Tokyo recruitment and partnership activities in their country
- Collaboration with other chapters on regional or continental initiatives
